Sit with your spine tall and your body relaxed but alert.
Let your hands rest on your knees with palms facing upward.
Close your eyes and take a few slow, deep breaths.
Feel the weight of your body settling downward into the earth.

Bring your attention to the very base of your spine.
This is the root, the foundation of your energy body.
Feel the area between your sitting bones and the base of your tailbone.
There is a warmth here, a density, a sense of something waiting.

Imagine a soft, warm glow at this root point.
It is the color of deep red, like embers in a quiet fire.
This energy has been with you since birth, steady and patient.
It simply needs your attention and your breath.

With each inhale, breathe gently down toward this root center.
Feel the breath touch the base of your spine like a warm hand.
With each exhale, feel the glow respond, brightening just slightly.
You are not pushing energy but inviting it to notice you.

Feel your connection to the earth through this root point.
You are grounded, stable, and safe.
From this place of security, the energy can begin to stir.

Now invite your awareness to rise to the space just below your navel.
This is the sacral center, the dwelling place of creativity and flow.
The color here shifts to a warm, glowing orange.
Feel a sensation of fluidity and warmth spreading through your lower belly.

Breathe into this orange glow and feel it expand.
The energy here moves like water, flowing and adaptable.
Your hips and lower back soften as this center comes alive.
Allow yourself to feel the natural vitality that lives here.

Now guide your awareness upward to the solar plexus, just above the navel.
This is the center of personal power and will.
A golden yellow light begins to glow here, warm as the sun.
Feel the strength and confidence that radiates from this center.

The fire here burns steadily, transforming intention into action.
Your upper abdomen feels warm, open, and energized.
You do not need to fight for your power, it simply is.
Breathe into the golden light and feel it strengthen.

Now let your awareness rise to the center of your chest.
This is the heart center, the meeting point of lower and upper energies.
A luminous green light opens here like a flower.
Feel the warmth and tenderness that fills your chest.

With each breath, the green light expands outward in all directions.
Your shoulders soften and your chest opens wider.
There is a sweetness here, a willingness to give and receive.
The heart is the bridge between the earthly and the vast.

Now bring your awareness to your throat.
This is the center of expression and truth.
A clear, sky-blue light begins to glow here.
Feel a gentle opening in the throat, as if space is being created for your voice.

The blue light vibrates gently, like a tuning fork struck softly.
Your jaw relaxes, your tongue softens, your neck grows long.
There is a freedom in this center, the freedom to speak your truth.
Breathe into the blue glow and feel it resonate through your whole neck.

Now guide your awareness to the space between your eyebrows.
This is the center of insight and inner vision.
A deep indigo light begins to glow here, rich and penetrating.
Feel a subtle pressure or tingling at this point.

This center sees beyond the surface of things.
It perceives patterns and meanings that the physical eyes cannot.
Thoughts grow quiet in the presence of this seeing.
You rest in a clarity that does not depend on words.

Now invite the energy to rise to the very crown of your head.
A violet light begins to glow here, luminous and vast.
It is not contained by the skull but extends upward without limit.
Feel the top of your head softening, opening, becoming transparent.

The violet light streams upward like a fountain into the space above.
There is a sense of expansion, of boundaries dissolving.
You are still yourself, but you are also part of something immeasurably larger.
The energy connects you to the sky, the stars, and the silence beyond them.

Rest here in this open, luminous space above the crown.
There is no edge, no wall, no limit.
The violet light mingles with pure white light that seems to come from everywhere.
Bathe in this light and let it wash through every cell of your body.

Now feel the entire column of energy alive within you simultaneously.
Red at the root, orange at the sacrum, yellow at the solar plexus.
Green at the heart, blue at the throat, indigo at the brow.
Violet at the crown, streaming into infinite white above.

Each center is connected to the ones above and below it.
Energy flows freely upward and downward through this central channel.
Your body feels alive and unified from root to crown.
You are a column of living energy, grounded in the earth and open to the sky.

Let the colors gradually soften and blend into one unified glow.
Your whole body is luminous, vibrating gently with life force.
This energy is always available to you, always flowing.
You have simply paused long enough to feel what was already there.

Begin to bring your awareness gently back to the room.
Feel the weight of your body, the touch of air on your skin.
Take a few deep breaths and allow movement to return to your fingers and toes.
Carry this sense of aliveness with you as you open your eyes.
