Welcome to this quiet time with your breath.

Allow your eyes to close and feel your body becoming still.

Notice the weight of your body resting fully on the surface beneath you.

Your hands are soft, your jaw is loose, your shoulders are easy.

Bring your full attention to the simple movement of your breathing.

Notice the cool sensation of air as it enters through your nostrils.

Follow that stream of air as it flows gently down into your chest.

Feel your ribcage expanding outward, soft and wide, with each inhale.

Your belly rises like a gentle wave as the breath fills you.

Just breathing.

Now notice the warm air as it flows back upward and outward.

Your belly settles, your chest softens, your body releases naturally.

Feel the brief, still pause that lives between each exhale and inhale.

A quiet moment of perfect rest exists right there in that pause.

Allow each breath to arrive at its own pace, its own depth.

Notice how your breath already knows exactly what to do.

Feel the gentle rhythm, steady and reliable, like ocean waves on shore.

Your whole body participates in this breathing, from belly to collarbone.

Even your back expands slightly with each full, easy inhale.

Notice the warmth that builds in your chest with each cycle of breath.

This warmth radiates outward through your whole body, soft and steady.

Your breath is your anchor, always here, always available to you.

Allow your awareness to rest gently on this rhythm, like a leaf on water.

Feel the aliveness that flows through you with every single breath.

Your body and your breath move together in quiet harmony.

With each cycle, a deeper sense of ease settles through you.

Begin to notice the sounds of the room returning around you.

Feel your body grounded and present, refreshed by this simple attention.

Take one slow, deep breath to mark this moment of peace.

Carry the steadiness of your breath with you as you gently open your eyes.
