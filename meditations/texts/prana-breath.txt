Welcome to this meditation on prana, the life force within your breath.

Allow your body to become still, resting in a comfortable position.

Feel the weight of your body held by the surface beneath you.

Notice your breath arriving and departing, like a gentle tide.

Each breath is alive with energy, flowing through every part of you.

Now bring your attention to the tip of your nose as you breathe in.

Feel a cool, fresh stream of air entering, carrying vitality with it.

Follow this breath as it travels down into your chest, warm and full.

Sense the gentle expansion of your ribs, welcoming this life force inward.

As breath flows out, feel a wave of warmth spreading through your body.

Your belly rises and falls like a calm sea, soft and rhythmic.

Each inhale draws golden energy upward along your spine.

Each exhale sends warm waves of ease flowing down through your limbs.

Feel prana tingling gently in your hands, alive and luminous.

Your feet receive this flowing energy, grounding you to the earth.

Now sense the breath filling your entire body at once, everywhere.

Prana moves freely through you, like light through clear water.

Every cell receives this nourishment, glowing with quiet vitality.

Your body breathes as one living whole, effortlessly sustained.

Allow the breath to find its own perfect rhythm, trusting its wisdom.

You are held by this life force, cradled in its steady current.

A deep sense of aliveness fills you from the inside outward.

Your whole being hums with this gentle, radiant energy.

Each breath renews you, fresh and warm and complete.

Begin to notice the sounds around you, returning gently to the room.

Feel the solid ground beneath you, steady and supporting.

Take one deep, nourishing breath, savoring this feeling of vitality.

Carry this living energy with you as you gently open your eyes.
