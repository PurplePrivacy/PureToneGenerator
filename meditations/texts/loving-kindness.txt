For this practice, it's especially important that we find a comfortable position.

Bring attention to breath at the belly, noticing sensations as the abdomen rises and falls during inhalation and exhalation.

Visualize someone who genuinely cares about your wellbeing, a friend, family member, teacher, or colleague. Imagine them truly wishing for you to be happy, fulfilled. For you to have a life that is flourishing.

Inhale to draw in that intention of goodness, recognizing that through our mind and imagination, it's as though we can call upon this valuable resource right here, right now.

After noticing emotional warmth from receiving kindness, extend this feeling outward. Bring to mind someone needing support and, just call upon this experience of wishing this person to be truly happy, fulfilled, joyful.

The practice concludes with three long inhalations and three long exhalations.
