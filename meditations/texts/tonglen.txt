Welcome to this practice of compassion, this generous exchange of breath.

Allow your eyes to close gently, feeling the weight of your body settle.

Notice the steady rhythm of your breathing, each cycle complete and whole.

Feel the warmth in the center of your chest, soft and glowing.

This warmth is your natural capacity for compassion, always present.

With each breath, this inner glow grows a little steadier, a little brighter.

Now as you breathe in, imagine drawing in something heavy, like gray smoke.

Feel it arrive in your chest, where the warmth immediately transforms it.

Your heart is vast enough to receive any heaviness and soften it completely.

As you breathe out, send bright, cool lightness flowing outward in all directions.

Each exhale carries ease and relief, like a cool breeze on a warm day.

Breathe in heaviness, feel it dissolve in the warmth of your chest.

Breathe out lightness, feeling it radiate from your body like soft sunlight.

Now bring to mind someone who is carrying a burden today.

As you breathe in, imagine drawing their heaviness gently toward your heart.

Feel your chest receive it with warmth, transforming it effortlessly.

As you breathe out, send them lightness, comfort, and spacious ease.

Each cycle of breath is a gift: receiving what is heavy, offering what is light.

Now widen this practice to include all beings who carry heaviness today.

Breathe in the weight of the world, feeling your vast heart receive it.

Breathe out gentleness and ease, flowing outward to every being everywhere.

Your compassion is boundless, your capacity to transform is limitless.

Now gently return the breath to its natural rhythm, easy and soft.

Feel the warmth still glowing in the center of your chest, steady and bright.

Your whole body rests in this field of generous, flowing compassion.

Each breath reminds you of the kindness you carry within.

Begin to notice the sounds around you, gently returning to the room.

Feel the surface beneath your body, solid and steady and warm.

Carry this compassionate warmth with you as you gently open your eyes.
