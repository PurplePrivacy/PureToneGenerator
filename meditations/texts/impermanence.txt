Welcome to this contemplation of change, this gentle noticing of flow.

Allow your eyes to close, and feel your body settling into this moment.

Notice the surface beneath you, steady and present and familiar.

Feel the breath arriving, filling your chest, then softly departing.

Each breath is unique, subtly different from the one before.

Even now, this moment is already becoming the next, flowing onward.

Bring your attention to the sensations in your hands, right now.

Notice the warmth, the tingling, the subtle aliveness in your fingers.

Observe how these sensations shift and change, even within a single breath.

The body is a river of sensation, always moving, always fresh.

Now notice the sounds around you, arriving and then fading away.

Each sound appears, lingers for a moment, and dissolves into silence.

This is the gentle rhythm of all experience: arising and passing.

Bring your awareness to your thoughts, watching them drift like clouds.

Each thought forms, floats across the sky of your mind, and moves on.

New thoughts arrive, each one different, each one temporary and light.

Now feel the temperature of the air on your skin, subtle and shifting.

Even this sensation changes moment to moment, always in gentle motion.

Everything you experience is part of this beautiful, ceaseless flow.

The breath changes, the body changes, the sounds around you change.

And within all this change, your awareness remains: steady, calm, present.

You are the still center within a world of gentle, flowing transformation.

Now allow your awareness to hold everything at once, all of it moving.

Feel the whole field of experience, alive and shifting and luminous.

There is something deeply restful in simply allowing everything to flow.

Each moment is a gift, arriving fresh and full and then moving on.

Begin to notice the room around you, gently returning to this place.

Feel the weight of your body, present and grounded and warm.

Carry this peaceful awareness of change with you as you open your eyes.
