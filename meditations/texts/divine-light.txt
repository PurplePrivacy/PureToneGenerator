Let your body come to rest in a position of complete comfort.
Feel the weight of your limbs growing heavy and still.
Allow the surface beneath you to hold you without any effort on your part.

Close your eyes and let the darkness behind your eyelids become soft and velvety.
Bring your attention to the rhythm of your breath, steady and natural.
Each inhale draws fresh energy into your body.
Each exhale releases anything that no longer serves you.

Now bring your awareness to the very top of your head, your crown.
Imagine the space just above you, quiet and luminous.
In that space, picture a sphere of warm, golden-white light.
It glows with a radiance that is both gentle and immensely powerful.

This light is not separate from you, it is your deepest essence made visible.
It has always been here, patient and constant, waiting for your attention.
Feel its warmth even before it touches you, like standing near a fire on a cold night.

Now let this light begin to descend, slowly, into the crown of your head.
Feel it enter like warm honey, soft and luminous and impossibly tender.
It fills your skull with radiance, dissolving tension you did not know you carried.
Your forehead smooths, your temples soften, the space behind your eyes glows.

The light moves down into your face, warming your cheekbones, your jaw, your lips.
Feel your jaw release its habitual holding as the light passes through.
Your mouth softens, your tongue rests, your whole face becomes luminous.

Now the light flows into your throat, warm and golden and healing.
It touches the place where words are formed and where truth is sometimes held back.
Feel your throat open and relax as the light fills every cell.

The warm radiance descends into your shoulders, those faithful bearers of burden.
Feel the light melt the tension in your shoulder muscles like sun melting frost.
It spreads down your arms, filling your upper arms, your elbows, your forearms.
It pools in your hands and fingertips, making them glow with gentle warmth.

Now feel the light enter your chest, your heart center.
This is where it seems to shine most brightly, as if it has found its true home.
Your heart fills with golden radiance, expanding with each breath.
Feel the warmth spreading through your ribs like sunlight through cathedral windows.

The light continues its descent into your belly, your center of gravity.
Feel it warm the organs that work silently to sustain your life.
Your stomach softens, your intestines relax, your whole midsection glows.
There is a feeling of deep nourishment, as if the light itself is food.

Now the radiance flows into your lower back and hips.
Feel the warmth reach those deep muscles that carry so much of your daily tension.
They soften and release as the golden light passes through them.
Your pelvis fills with warm luminosity, stable and grounded.

The light moves down through your thighs, strong and warm.
It flows past your knees, filling your calves, your shins, your ankles.
It pools in your feet and toes, making them glow with the same gentle radiance.
You are now filled from crown to sole with warm, golden-white light.

Take a moment to feel your entire body as a single column of radiance.
Every cell is illuminated, every tissue is warm, every organ glows.
You are not a body containing light, you are light that has taken the shape of a body.

Now imagine this inner light beginning to expand beyond your skin.
It seeps outward like dawn spreading across a dark landscape.
A soft, golden aura forms around your body, just a few inches at first.
Then it grows wider, surrounding you in a cocoon of warm luminosity.

This light extends a foot beyond you, then two, then fills the room.
It passes through walls and ceilings as easily as sunlight passes through glass.
Nothing can contain it, nothing can diminish it, nothing can extinguish it.

Feel yourself resting in the center of this vast, warm radiance.
You are both the source and the ocean of this light.
It is as intimate as your heartbeat and as vast as the sky.

Now imagine that this light connects you to a source far greater than yourself.
Above you, the light extends upward without limit, joining a universal radiance.
Below you, it reaches into the earth, connecting to the warm core of the planet.
You are a bridge between heaven and earth, a channel for something infinite.

Feel the current of this universal light flowing through you.
It enters through your crown and exits through your feet into the earth.
It rises from the earth and pours upward through you toward the sky.
You are not generating this light, you are simply allowing it to move through you.

Rest in this flow, this effortless channeling of universal radiance.
There is nothing to do, nothing to achieve, nothing to become.
You are already what the light is, you have always been this.

With each breath, feel the light pulse a little brighter.
With each heartbeat, feel your connection to the infinite grow stronger.
You are luminous, radiant, boundless.

As this meditation gently comes to a close, let the light settle into a steady glow.
It does not leave you when you open your eyes, it simply becomes quieter.
It is always within you, always shining, always available.

Take one more breath and feel the warmth of this light in your heart.
You carry it with you into every room, every conversation, every moment.
You are the light, and the light is you.
