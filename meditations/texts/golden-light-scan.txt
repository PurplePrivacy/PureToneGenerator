Let your body come to rest in whatever position feels most welcoming.
Allow the surface beneath you to take your full weight.
Close your eyes and let the world outside grow quiet.

Feel the natural movement of your breath.
Let it be easy, without any effort to change it.

Now bring your attention down to the very tips of your toes.
Notice whatever sensations are present there.
This is where the journey begins.

Imagine a warm golden light beginning to glow at the tips of your toes.
It is soft and radiant, like the first light of sunrise.
It carries healing and comfort in its gentle luminescence.

Feel the golden light spreading into the soles of your feet.
Wherever the light touches, tension dissolves like mist in morning sun.
Your feet glow softly from within, warm and completely at ease.

The light moves upward into your ankles.
Any stiffness in the ankle joints softens in the golden glow.

Now the warm light rises into your calves and shins.
It flows through the muscles like honey, slow and thorough.
Your lower legs glow with the same warm radiance as your feet.

The golden light reaches your knees and wraps around each one like a warm compress.
These joints that bend and flex so faithfully are honored by the light.

The light continues upward into your thighs.
Feel the golden warmth spreading through the full thickness of the tissue.
Your thighs become heavy and warm, deeply relaxed in the glow.

Now the light reaches your hips and pelvis.
This area often holds tension it has carried for a long time.
The golden light finds it and gently, patiently, dissolves it.

Feel the light moving into your lower belly.
It fills the soft space of your abdomen with gentle warmth.
Any holding or tightness simply lets go.

The golden light rises along your lower back.
It flows between and around each vertebra of the lumbar spine.
Your lower back glows with the same soft light that began in your toes.

The warmth continues upward through your middle back.
It wraps around your ribs like a warm band of comfort.
Your breathing feels easier as these muscles soften in the glow.

Now the golden light enters your chest.
It fills the space around your heart with extraordinary warmth.
Your chest opens and expands as the light dissolves any constriction there.

The warmth flows into your upper back and between your shoulder blades.
The golden light melts layer after layer of tension there.
Feel the knots releasing, the muscles spreading, the tissue softening.

The light rises into your shoulders like a warm mantle.
Feel the weight lifting as the golden radiance fills every fiber.
Your shoulders settle into deep ease.

Now the light flows down through both arms simultaneously.
It passes through your elbows, into your forearms, your wrists.
The golden light pours into your hands and flows into each finger.
Your hands are luminous, warm, and deeply relaxed.

The light returns to your center and rises into your throat.
Any tightness or constriction there dissolves.
Your neck softens in the golden glow.

The warm light flows into your jaw and the muscles release their habitual grip.
It rises into your cheeks, around your eyes, warming the delicate muscles.

The golden light flows into your temples and across your forehead.
Any worry lines dissolve in the warmth.
Your forehead becomes smooth and luminous.

Now the golden light rises to the very crown of your head.
The light has traveled from the tips of your toes to the top of your head.
Your entire body is illuminated from within.

Feel what it is like to glow from head to toe.
Every cell is bathed in warm golden light.
You are a body of light, warm and whole and deeply at peace.

Feel the steady pulse of the golden glow synchronized with your breath.
Inhale and the light brightens across your whole body.
Exhale and it softens to a warm, steady radiance.

Now let the light radiate gently beyond your skin.
A soft golden aura forms around your entire body.
You are surrounded by your own healing radiance.

Rest here for a moment in this luminous stillness.
Tension has dissolved, heaviness has lifted, warmth has settled in.

Know that this light does not leave when the meditation ends.
Every cell remembers the warmth it received.
You carry this radiance with you wherever you go.

Let your eyes open slowly, as if waking from a warm, golden dream.
The light is in you now, and it is yours to keep.
