Allow your body to settle into whatever position feels most comfortable right now.
Let the weight of your body be fully supported by the surface beneath you.
There is nothing you need to do in this moment but rest.

Feel the gentle rhythm of your breath as it moves through you.
Each inhale arrives without effort, a quiet gift your body gives itself.
Each exhale carries you a little deeper into stillness.

Begin to notice the warmth where your body meets the surface below.
Feel the soft pressure of contact, the way gravity holds you in place.
You are held, completely and without condition.

Now bring your attention to your hands, resting wherever they have settled.
Notice the subtle pulse of life in your fingertips.
These hands have touched so many things, held so many people, created so much.
Let a feeling of quiet gratitude arise for everything your hands have done.

Feel into your feet, those faithful companions on every journey you have taken.
They have carried you through doorways, across thresholds, into new chapters.
Allow a warm appreciation to spread through the soles of your feet.

Bring your awareness to your eyes, even as they rest closed.
Think of the colors, the faces, the landscapes they have shown you.
So much beauty has passed through these two small windows.
Let gratitude for the gift of sight glow softly behind your eyelids.

Notice your ears and the world of sound they open for you.
Music, laughter, the voice of someone who loves you.
Even now they carry the gentle sound of your own breathing to your awareness.

Feel the air entering your nostrils, cool and fresh on the inhale.
Your breath has been with you since the very first moment of your life.
It has never once abandoned you, never taken a single break.
Let yourself feel grateful for this most faithful companion of all.

Now allow your heart to soften as you think of someone who has loved you well.
It does not matter when or how, only that the love was real.
Picture their face, the warmth in their eyes when they looked at you.
Feel how that love still lives inside your body, still warms you from within.

Perhaps there are others who come to mind, people who have shaped you.
A teacher who believed in you before you believed in yourself.
A friend who sat with you in silence when words were not enough.
A stranger whose small kindness changed the course of your day.
Let gratitude for each of them bloom like a slow, warm light in your chest.

Now widen the circle of your gratitude to include your own life story.
Every experience, even the difficult ones, has brought you to this moment.
The challenges carved your strength, the losses deepened your compassion.
You are richer for everything you have lived through.
Allow yourself to appreciate the fullness of your own journey.

Turn your attention now to the natural world that surrounds your life.
The sun that warms your skin on an ordinary afternoon.
Rain that feeds the roots of trees you will never see fully grown.
The steady turning of seasons, each one arriving exactly on time.
The night sky with its ancient, patient stars.
Feel how you belong to something vast and beautiful and alive.

Notice now the simple miracle of being conscious in this moment.
You are aware of yourself, aware of your breath, aware of this gratitude.
Billions of years of unfolding led to this quiet instant of your awareness.
What an extraordinary thing it is simply to exist.

Let the feeling of gratitude expand beyond any single object or person.
Let it become a warm, golden glow that fills your entire body.
Feel it in your belly, your chest, your throat, your face.
Let it radiate outward from your skin like gentle heat from a candle.

You do not need a reason to feel grateful.
Gratitude can simply be a way of being, a quality of attention.
It is available to you in every moment, in every breath.

As this meditation begins to draw to a close, take one more full breath.
Feel the gratitude settle into your bones like sunlight warming stone.
Know that you can return to this feeling whenever you choose.
It is always here, always waiting, always yours.

Carry this warmth with you as you gently return to your day.
Let it color the way you see, the way you listen, the way you touch.
The world is full of gifts, and you are one of them.
