Settle into a position of comfort and let your body be still.
Feel the generous support of the surface beneath you.
It gives without being asked, it holds without being thanked.
This is how abundance works, quietly, constantly, without condition.

Bring your attention to your breath and notice its effortless arrival.
You did not earn this breath, you did not plan for it, and yet it came.
Another follows, and another, an endless procession of gifts.
Your next inhale is already forming itself, already on its way to you.

Consider for a moment the sheer abundance of breath in your lifetime.
Hundreds of millions of breaths, each one freely given.
Not a single one has ever been withheld from you.
This is the foundation of all abundance, the fact that you are breathing right now.

Now expand your attention to include the sensations in your body.
Feel the warmth in your hands, the weight of your limbs, the pulse in your wrists.
Notice the thousands of sensations available to you in this single moment.
Tingling, pressure, temperature, texture, aliveness moving through every cell.

You are a symphony of sensation, and every note is playing right now.
The richness of being in a body is staggering when you pause to notice it.
This is not scarcity, this is overwhelming, miraculous fullness.

Listen now to the sounds around you, near and far.
Perhaps the hum of air, the distant movement of life, your own quiet breathing.
Sound arrives from every direction, pouring into your ears without effort.
The world is constantly offering itself to your awareness.

Even silence is full, a rich, textured presence rather than an absence.
There is no moment in your life that is truly empty.
Every instant brims with more sensation than you could ever catalog.

Feel into the space your body occupies, the air touching your skin from all sides.
Consider that this air contains the molecules of every breath ever taken.
You share this atmosphere with every person who has ever lived.
The very air you breathe has traveled through ancient forests and across wide oceans.

Now bring your attention to your heart and the life that pulses there.
Feel the steady rhythm of abundance, beat after beat after beat.
Your heart has pumped enough blood to fill a lake, all without your asking.
It gives and gives and gives, the most generous organ you possess.

Let a feeling of gratitude arise for what already exists in your life.
Not what you wish for, not what is missing, but what is here right now.
The ability to feel, to breathe, to hear, to think, to love.
These are not small things, they are the entire treasure.

Think of the people who populate your life, whether many or few.
Each one is a universe of experience choosing to share time with you.
The conversations, the laughter, the quiet moments of simply being together.
Every relationship is an abundance that cannot be measured or counted.

Consider the food that has nourished your body throughout your life.
Seeds that grew into plants, rain that fed the soil, hands that harvested and prepared.
An unbroken chain of generosity stretching from the earth to your table.
You have been fed, literally and figuratively, every single day of your life.

Think of the knowledge and beauty that human beings have created.
Music, stories, art, discoveries, all freely available, all abundantly offered.
You live in the richest library of human experience ever assembled.
More beauty exists than any one life could ever absorb.

Now let your awareness expand to the natural world in all its excess.
Stars beyond counting, grains of sand beyond number, species beyond naming.
Nature does not practice moderation, it creates with wild, reckless generosity.
Millions of seeds for every tree, billions of stars for every sky.

You are made of the same substance as that generosity.
Your body is built from stardust, your blood runs with ocean salt.
You are not separate from the abundance of the universe, you are an expression of it.
The same force that fills the night sky with stars fills your lungs with breath.

Rest in this recognition for a few quiet breaths.
You are not a small, limited thing hoping for scraps from an indifferent universe.
You are the universe itself, experiencing its own abundance through your senses.
Everything you have ever needed has found its way to you.

Feel the fullness of this moment, the absolute completeness of right now.
Nothing is missing, nothing is insufficient, nothing needs to be added.
This breath is complete, this heartbeat is complete, this moment is complete.

Let this feeling of fullness spread through your entire body like warm light.
Feel it in your belly, steady and nourishing.
Feel it in your chest, expansive and free.
Feel it in your hands, ready to give as generously as you have received.

Abundance is not something you achieve, it is something you notice.
It has been here all along, faithful and patient and overflowing.
Your only task is to open your eyes and let yourself see it.

Take one more full, grateful breath and feel the richness of being alive.
This breath is proof enough that the universe wants you here.
Carry this awareness of abundance gently back into your waking life.
Everything you need is already on its way.
