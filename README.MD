# Resonance ‚Äî Therapeutic Audio Engine

Your personal sound therapist, running entirely on your machine. No cloud, no microphone, no external audio ‚Äî just pure mathematics turned into healing sound.

---

### What You Get

**Scientifically-Grounded Breathing** ‚Äî 6 HRV breath pacing patterns (box breathing, 4-7-8, and more) with a live terminal breathing bar and 8 handcrafted audio cues (singing bowl, bell, woodblock...) to guide every inhale and exhale.

**Therapeutic Voice Sessions** ‚Äî Up to 500+ counter-conditioning affirmations across 27 expert-reviewed phases, covering somatic restoration, nervous system regulation, identity reclamation, default-state conditioning, grace, purification, body scan, and cellular healing. Available in English and French with Amelie positive reinforcement voice.

**EMDR-Style Bilateral Stimulation** ‚Äî Smooth alternating left/right audio for bilateral processing. Combine with voice alternation (`--alternate`) for full EMDR-inspired sessions where affirmations move between ears.

**Solfeggio & Focus Frequencies** ‚Äî Tunable from 174 Hz (grounding) to 852 Hz (clarity), with optional 40 Hz gamma isochronic pulses for deep focus and study.

**739-Book Audiobook Library** ‚Äî Listen to Marcus Aurelius, Dostoyevsky, Hugo, Voltaire, Jules Verne, H.G. Wells, Proust, Balzac, Austen, Dickens, Shakespeare, Herodotus, Gibbon, Michelet, and 700+ more classics read aloud during your sessions. 277 French, 462 English ‚Äî covering philosophy, sci-fi, fantasy, classics, history, and personal improvement. Page tracking, resume support, and real-time subtitles.

**Intelligent Reading Rhythm** ‚Äî Audiobooks play with text-aware pacing by default: punctuation-sensitive pause hierarchy (periods > semicolons > commas), word-count breath pauses at natural intervals, and glue-word protection to keep articles attached to their nouns. Disable with `--no-rhythm` for raw TTS speed.

**20 One-Flag Presets** ‚Äî From `--sleep` to `--emdr-session` to `--morning-energy`, each preset is a complete therapeutic session in a single command. No configuration needed.

**30-Minute Fade-to-Silence** ‚Äî Gradual volume dissolution for sleep, meditation, or power naps. Fall asleep to sound; wake up to silence.

**FLAC Export** ‚Äî Save any session as a 1-hour lossless audio file for offline listening.

---

### Untamperable by Design

Every sample that reaches your ears is **mathematically computed in-process**:

- **Single output stream only** ‚Äî one `sounddevice.OutputStream`, no input, no duplex
- **No external audio sources** ‚Äî no file playback, no network audio
- **No input devices** ‚Äî microphone access is architecturally impossible
- **Deterministic** ‚Äî fixed RNG seed, reproducible across runs
- **Cryptographic integrity proof** (`--integrity`) ‚Äî rolling SHA-256 of every buffer
- **Lockdown mode** (`--lockdown`) ‚Äî pure sine + output-only + integrity combined

#### Reading Rhythm (enabled by default)

Audiobooks play with intelligent pacing that mimics a skilled human narrator:

- **Punctuation-aware pauses** ‚Äî periods get 450ms, semicolons/colons 250ms, commas 150ms, dashes 180ms. French text gets a 15% multiplier for naturally longer phrasing.
- **Word-count breath pauses** ‚Äî 1-second micro-silences are inserted at word positions 1, 3, 5, 9 (cycling within each sentence), creating a natural breathing cadence.
- **Cycling character thresholds** ‚Äî additional pause points at 12, 18, 25 character intervals (cycling) for even pacing density.
- **Glue-word protection** ‚Äî articles, prepositions, and short connectors (EN + FR) are never orphaned from their noun. "The" won't be separated from "book."
- **Aggregate cap** ‚Äî no more than 2 seconds of added silence per sentence, preventing over-extension on long sentences.
- **Safe insertion** ‚Äî pauses are only placed in confirmed silent regions (5% RMS threshold, 60ms minimum gap), with 15ms safety margins and 5ms crossfades to prevent any speech clipping.

To disable rhythm and use raw TTS output:
```bash
python stream_tone.py --audiobook meditations --no-rhythm
```

## HRV Breathing Styles

**Simple (2-phase):**
- **A** ‚Äî Symmetric (5.5s in / 5.5s out) ‚Äî classic HRV coherence
- **B** ‚Äî Parasympathetic (4s in / 6.5s out) ‚Äî deeper relaxation
- **C** ‚Äî Deep meditative (6s in / 6s out) ‚Äî very slow, deep calm

**Controlled (with hold phases):**
- **box** ‚Äî Box breathing (4-4-4-4) ‚Äî stress control
- **478** ‚Äî 4-7-8 breathing (4s in / 7s hold / 8s out) ‚Äî relaxation technique
- **426** ‚Äî 4-2-6 breathing (4s in / 2s hold / 6s out) ‚Äî calm focus

### Real-Time Text Display

The current sentence is logged to the console as it plays, so you can follow along if you miss a word:

```
  > Marcus Aurelius began each morning by telling himself...
  > The happiness of your life depends upon the quality of your thoughts.
```

### Page Tracking & Resume

Every 10 sentences, page progress is logged. On Ctrl-C, you get a resume command:

```
  [Candide ‚Äî Voltaire [FR]] page 5/120
  Resume with: --audiobook candide --audiobook-page 5
```

With `--audiobook-resume`, progress is auto-saved and restored between sessions.
